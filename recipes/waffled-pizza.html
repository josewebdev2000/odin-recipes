Waffled Pizza

Go Back to Main Page

Description

If you are somebody who loves brunch, then this weird recipe is perfect for you.

The best breakfast dish ever combined with the best lunch dish ever.

Who would complain?

Ingredients

  • 7/4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cups shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
  • 1 (14 ounce) jar pizza sauce
  • 32 small pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Heat waffle maker with 8-inch-square cooking surface on Medium-High heat setting
  2. In medium bowl, mix both cheeses, basil and oregano; set aside
  3. In 1 1/2-quart saucepan, heat pizza sauce over low heat, stirring occasionally, until hot. Place in small serving bowl
  4. Unroll both cans of dough onto work surface. Separate each crosswise, making four (7-by-6-inch) rectangles; firmly press perforations to seal
  5. Place 1 dough rectangle on bottom surface of heated waffle maker, being careful not to open up perforations. Quickly sprinkle 1 cup cheese mixture evenly on top of dough. Place half the slices of pepperoni evenly over cheese mixture. Top with another dough rectangle; close waffle maker. Bake 2 to 3 minutes or until sandwich is golden brown
  6. With pancake turner, remove from waffle maker; place on cutting board. Cool 30 seconds before cutting
  7. Cut into square sandwiches or triangular snacks
  8. Repeat with remaining dough, cheese mixture and pepperoni
  9. Serve warm with pizza sauce for dipping
